Our Story

Built From Real Experience

Alex Road Service was founded on one principle: keep commercial vehicles moving. The company was built by technicians who knew firsthand how costly downtime is for owner-operators and fleet businesses alike.

What started as a mobile repair operation quickly grew into a full-service commercial truck repair shop with multiple mobile service units, a dedicated service bay, and a team of experienced diesel technicians.

Today, Alex Road Service serves fleet managers, dispatchers, owner-operators, and commercial clients throughout Woodbridge, Keasbey, and the greater New Jersey area — providing fast, reliable onsite truck and trailer repair.
