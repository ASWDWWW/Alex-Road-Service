FAQ

Payment Questions

Do you require payment upfront for roadside service?

For emergency roadside calls, we may require a card on file or deposit for parts before dispatch. Shop repairs are typically invoiced upon completion. Fleet account customers are invoiced monthly.

Can I pay my invoice online?

Yes. We can send a secure payment link by email or text. Click the link, enter your card details, and you'll receive a paid receipt immediately.

Do you offer Net-30 terms?

Net-30 terms are available for established fleet accounts. Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll work out a billing arrangement that makes sense for your business.

Is financing available for major repairs?

We are actively working on third-party financing options for major repair jobs. Contact us to discuss your situation — we want to find a solution that keeps your truck on the road without putting you in a difficult financial position.
