Diesel Engine Service & Repair

From diagnostics to full overhauls, we service all major commercial diesel engines. Our technicians have hands-on experience with fuel systems, injection, turbocharging, and engine management systems.

  • Engine diagnostics & fault code analysis
  • Fuel injection system service & repair
  • Turbocharger inspection, rebuild, or replacement
  • EGR & DPF service
  • Cylinder head & gasket repair
  • Full engine rebuild & overhaul

Brake Systems
Brakes & Air Systems

Air Brake Service & Inspection

We perform complete air brake system service from inspection to repair. We understand DOT brake compliance and can get your truck through inspection — and keep it there.

  • Brake shoe & lining replacement
  • Brake chamber & spring brake service
  • S-cam & disc brake repair
  • Automatic brake adjuster (ABA) service
  • Air compressor & dryer replacement
  • Full brake inspection & adjustment
