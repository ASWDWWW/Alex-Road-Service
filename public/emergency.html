What We Fix

On-Site Emergency Repairs

Our mobile service trucks carry the tools and parts to handle most breakdowns right where you stopped. No tow required in most cases.

Tire & Blowout Service
Steer, drive, and trailer tire changes. We carry commercial truck tires and can mount and torque on-site. No waiting for a wrecker.
Air System Failures
Brake chamber replacements, air line leaks, gladhands, air dryer, and compressor diagnostics. We restore braking quickly and safely.
Battery & Electrical
Jump starts, battery replacement, alternator checks, and no-start diagnostics. We carry commercial batteries for same-day replacement.
Brake Failures
Emergency brake repairs including brake shoe replacement, brake adjustment, and chamber service to restore safe stopping ability.
Hydraulic Hose Repair
On-site hose fabrication and replacement for dump trucks, liftgates, and any hydraulic-equipped commercial vehicle.
Diagnostics & Fault Code Reading
Our techs carry diagnostic tools for all major platforms. We'll read codes, identify the problem, and advise on the fastest path to a fix.
Fuel & DEF Service
Ran out of fuel or DEF? We can assist with emergency fuel delivery and DEF refill to get you moving until the next stop.
Lighting & Harness Repair
Broken truck or trailer lights causing a roadside inspection risk? We repair lighting, wiring harnesses, and connectors on-site.
